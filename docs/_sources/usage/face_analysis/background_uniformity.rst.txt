.. _background_uniformity:

=======================================
Background uniformity check and removal
=======================================

The :ref:`FaceAnalyser.computeBackgroundUniformity<face_analyser_compute_background_uniformity_class_method>` method analyses the :ref:`color uniformity<background_uniformity_color_uniformity_struct_member>` and :ref:`structure uniformity<background_uniformity_structure_uniformity_struct_member>` of the background in a portrait image. A high score means that the background is uniform. 

Background removal should be performed in two steps:

#. Call the :ref:`FaceAnalyser.segmentFace<face_analyser_segment_face_class_method>` method to create a segmentation mask.
#. Call the :ref:`FaceAnalyser.applySegmentationMask<face_analyser_apply_segmentation_mask_class_method>` method to apply the segmentation mask and replace the background by a specified color.

.. important:: 
    The FaceBackgroundUniformity1A model is required to check the background uniformity.
    The FaceSegmenter1A model is required to suppress the background.


Example
-------
The example below checks the background uniformity around a detected face and supress the background.

.. tab-set::

    .. tab-item:: C#
    
       .. code-block:: c#
    
            FaceAnalyser analyser = new FaceAnalyser();

            analyser.ComputeBackgroundUniformity();
