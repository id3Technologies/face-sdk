.. _age_estimation:

==============
Age estimation
==============

Landmarks such as the location of the pupils, eye corners, lip boundaries, etc. change with age.

|product-name| was trained on a large database of different faces to detect the approximate age of a person based on such features.

- Age estimation accuracy up to +/- 5 years.
- Accuracy depends on conditions such as lighting, head pose, etc.

.. note:: Required AI model: **FaceAgeEstimator1A**
