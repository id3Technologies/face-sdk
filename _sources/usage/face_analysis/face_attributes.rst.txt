.. _face_attributes:

===========================
Face attributes computation
===========================

The :ref:`face_analyser_class` can estimate in one step a group of facial attributes, including:

- Glasses 
- Hat 
- Make-up 
- Male (gender estimation)
- Mouth opening 
- Smile 

.. note:: Required AI model: **FaceAttributesClassifier2A**
